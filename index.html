A Generational Asset

A riverfront heritage site whose development envelope expands materially under sovereign jurisdiction.

The former Olympia Brewery sits at the head of the planned Deschutes Estuary restoration, anchored by a 1906 brewhouse that is among the most photographed industrial buildings in the South Sound. Under municipal jurisdiction, the site carries significant critical area, frontage, and zoning constraints. Under tribal trust status, the regulatory layer that drives most of the engineering cost falls away — while federal environmental review, treaty fishing protections, and heritage preservation continue to govern. The same physical site supports a substantially larger program.
