Ops

Day-to-Day Operations

Comprehensive operating oversight across hospitality, event, residential, and F&B verticals. Single management interface to the tribal enterprise. Coordination with tribal employment, vendor preference, and procurement policies.

Fin.

Financial Management & Reporting

Monthly financial package delivered by the 15th: balance sheet, income statement, budget vs. actual, A/R and A/P, bank reconciliations. Customized reporting aligned with the tribal enterprise’s accounting and treasury preferences. Year-end audit coordination.

Rev.

Leasing & Revenue

For the residential component: full leasing platform including market positioning, automated inquiry workflow, in-person and virtual tours, application and screening, digital lease execution, and make-ready coordination. Hospitality and event revenue managed through specialized platforms.

CapEx

Facilities & Capital Planning

In-house Construction & Facilities team supporting preventive maintenance, capital project oversight, multi-year capital forecasting, and 24/7 emergency response. Particularly relevant given the operational complexity of an adaptively reused heritage property.

Tech

Owner Portal & Reporting Platform

Real-time dashboard with live financial and operational reporting. Secure banking for distributions and contributions. Investment performance metrics. Document management. Architectural and capital approval workflow. Same platform used in the active tribal engagement.

Gov.

Tribal Enterprise Coordination

Direct reporting line to the tribal enterprise board or designated governance body. Quarterly in-person briefings, monthly operational updates, immediate escalation for material events. AGM functions as an extension of the tribal enterprise, not as an external vendor.

Fee Structure: Two-part structure consistent with AGM’s standard hospitality and multifamily engagements: monthly retainer (percentage of gross revenues, minimum threshold) plus one-time onboarding. Capital projects coordinated on hourly or percentage-of-project-cost basis with prior approval. Detailed term sheet available under NDA.
